== Description ==

Add seamless transition effects between your pages with different captivating effects.
Add personalized loaders or your logo, customize your cursor, or even implement a whimsical confetti effect on your forms. AnimatePress makes all this possible

**Demos**
[Click to see a demo of all transitions](https://www.animatepress.net/demo/)

**Free version features**

5 transitions effects available :

*   Fade effect
*   Slide to top effect
*   Slide to right effect
*   Slide to bottom effect
*   Slide to left effect
*   Choose the color of your transition effect

Add a loader to your transition

*   Spinner effect
*   Choose the color of your spinner

**Pro version features**

*   Transitions : unlock more than 20 effects.
*   Transitions : select your custom color.
*   Transitions : Customize waiting and timing transitions.
*   Loaders : unlock more than 10 loaders.
*   Loaders : select your custom color.
*   Loaders : customize loader with your own logo.
*   Confettis : Add a confetti effect to your pages using a shortcode or Gutenberg block with 5 different styles.
*   Cursors : Customize your cursor by choosing its shape and color, adding a modern touch to your website.

== Installation ==

**With WordPress dashboard**

*   From the [Administration Screen](http://codex.wordpress.org/Administration_Panels), click on the [Plugins](http://codex.wordpress.org/Administration_Panels#Plugins) section
*   click the *Add New* sub menu
*   Click on *Upload plugin*, and select the zip file you previously downloaded.
*   Then click the *Install Now* button
*   After installation and activation, you will find a new menu item called *AnimatePress*

**With SFTP client**

*   Connect to your server
*   Upload the zip file folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
*   Activate the plugin in the [Plugins](http://codex.wordpress.org/Administration_Panels#Plugins) Menu
*   After installation and activation, you will find a new menu item called *AnimatePress*

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What are the requirements for the plugin to work properly ? =

Minimum requirements are PHP 7.0 and WordPress 6.0. Use PHP 8.x for better performances and security reasons. Also, make sure that your theme uses the [wp_body_open tag](https://developer.wordpress.org/reference/functions/wp_body_open/). Otherwise, AnimatePress will not function.

= Do I need technical development knowledge ? =

Not at all, let yourself be guided by the various configuration menus. If you have any hesitation or questions, don’t hesitate to consult [the documentation or video tutorials](https://www.animatepress.net/documentation/).

= Could your plugin slow down my site ? =

No, all animations are designed to minimize the impact on your site’s performance.
